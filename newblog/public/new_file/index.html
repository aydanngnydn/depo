# Summary of AI Cameras and Road Safety Measures on A361

## Context
Artificial intelligence (AI) cameras are being implemented on the A361 road near Frome, Somerset, to address the rising number of serious crashes. In less than two years, nine fatalities have occurred on this road.

## AI Camera Features
- The cameras can detect:
  - Motorists texting while driving
  - Speed limit violations
  - Seatbelt usage

## Police Actions 
- Avon and Somerset Police have reported 22 fatal or serious injury incidents on the A361 in 2023.
- Acting Insp. Matt Boiles expressed the emotional toll of informing families about fatalities and emphasized the need for public safety.
- A "day of action" included unmarked police cars and speed detection efforts.

## Family Impact
- Following the death of sisters Madison (21) and Liberty North (17) in a high-speed crash, their parents voiced feelings of being "let down" by continued accidents.
- They advocated for speed control measures on the A361 to prevent future tragedies.

## Planned Safety Improvements
- In collaboration with local MP Anna Sabine, new safety measures will be introduced, including:
  - Improved signs and road markings
  - Vegetation management for better visibility

Ms. Sabine highlighted the importance of awareness regarding road safety behaviors in the area's countryside.

## Conclusion
The introduction of AI technology and additional safety measures aims to reduce road incidents on the A361, enhancing both public confidence and safety on a route with a troubling accident record.
