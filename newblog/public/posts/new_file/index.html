Summary of AI Cameras Implemented to Enhance Road Safety #

Introduction #

Avon and Somerset Police are deploying artificial intelligence cameras on the A361 near Frome, Somerset, to address a rise in serious accidents, which have resulted in nine fatalities over the past two years.

Background #

The A361 has seen 22 fatal or serious injury incidents in 2023. The new AI cameras will monitor for texting while driving, speeding, and seatbelt use, marking a first for the county.

Police Actions #

Acting Inspector Matt Boiles highlighted the devastating impact of these accidents on families and the police force. Unmarked police cars and speed detection equipment were utilized during a recent “day of action” to tackle the increase in incidents.

Family Concerns #

Parents Verity and Jason North, who lost their daughters in a high-speed crash, expressed feelings of being let down due to continued fatalities on the A361. They emphasized that stronger speed controls could prevent future tragedies, noting reckless driving practices in the area.

Additional Safety Measures #

Following advocacy from local MP Anna Sabine, further safety measures are planned, including better signage, improved road markings, and vegetation management to enhance visibility.

Conclusion #

The aim of implementing these measures is to enhance public safety and confidence in road networks and to prevent further trauma for affected families and the police workforce.
